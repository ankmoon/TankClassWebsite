The Problem with Modern Roadmaps

Most product roadmaps are just glorified wishlists or hidden Gantt charts. They break the moment the first engineering sprint hits a snag, and they fail to communicate the actual *value* being delivered to the business.

In this class, we leverage the Tank Perspective: building resilient roadmaps that focus on outcomes rather than features. We'll dive deep into the intersection of market signals, user needs, and technical constraints.
